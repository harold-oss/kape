what's make our coffee special!

The ratio of ingredients is what separates the two drinks; however: while cappuccinos have equal ratios, lattes have more steamed milk. The ingredients in lattes are also mixed together instead of being layered, like what can be found in cappuccinos. Compared to cappuccinos and the many other espresso-based drinks, lattes are not very strong in taste. This is because they are heavier with milk than coffee. Due to being milkier than their counterparts, they are sweeter than most coffee, though still not as sweet as you will find in mochas.
